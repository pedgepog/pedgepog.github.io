Gold Cage

Gold Cage is a stats-management game in development about the price of maintaining power to protect the ones you love.

A duchess has long been pressured by the Queen to marry a noble man. There's one problem - she's in love with her female knight and childhood friend.

She has 3 days until she is officially married. Increasing her stats will convince the Queen that she doesn't need marriage to maintain her power as a woman, unlocking the choice to reject the engagement.

But what she doesn't know is that if she calls off the engagement, the noble man will kill her.
