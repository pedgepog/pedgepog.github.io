Dating Show Dating Sim

Dating Show Dating Sim is a game in development, drawing heavily from Korean dating shows. Our main character, an heiress girl, appears on a Bachelor-like dating show to find a husband. The catch? One's painfully narcissistic, one's uncaring and businesslike, one's a stalker, and one's just painfully uncommitted.

You play as the female producer of the show, the person whom our main character seeks help from. Your job is to guide her actions through this mess.

But as her only source of emotional refuge in this disaster of a courting ritual, don't be surprised if you and our main character become more... emotionally entangled than you would have expected.
